Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings: Settings & Privacy → Settings
  2. Click on "Apps and Websites"
  3. Find "DreamBased" app in the list
  4. Click "Remove" to delete all app data

Alternative Method

Send email to [twój-email@gmail.com] with subject "Data Deletion Request" and we will remove your data within 7 days.

What Gets Deleted

  • All stored authentication tokens
  • Any analytics data related to your account
  • Access permissions to your Facebook/Instagram accounts
